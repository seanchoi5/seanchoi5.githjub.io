The layout of this page was inspired by the Arcangel Surfware. I replicate the look of Cory's website to make it look as identical to the original as possible by composing the dashboard on the lower left corner with the text reading vertically upwards, making the body text align to the right, and including the Arcangel Surfware logo on the upper right. As multiple HTML pages were required for this project, instead of making the links in the navigation bar taking you to different parts of one body of text, I made it so that it takes you to another page generated by separate HTML files. The styling of the pages will remain consistent as they all refer to the same styling sheet and the navigation bar will reconfigure to differing links from the page you are currently visiting to avoid redundancy.
